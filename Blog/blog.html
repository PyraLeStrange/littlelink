Beef Stew Seasoning

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp onion powder
  • 2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp celery seed
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1⁄2 tsp cayenne pepper

Recommendations:

  • Use 4 Tbsp per pound of beef stew

  • 2 cups of water in pressure cooker on STEW setting with a small bag of baby carrots, 3 large potatoes, 1 large onion sliced, and 1 large green pepper sliced

  • Optional: Can also be done by itself and served with cooked rice or noodles or pasta

    Homemade Fried KFC Chicken

    Best baked chicken that you'll swear it's fried!

    Ingredients:

    KFC Seasoning Ingredients:
    • 1⁄2 cup chili powder
    • 3 Tbsp cumin
    • 1 Tbsp salt
    • 1 Tbsp pepper
    • 1 Tbsp onion flakes
    • 1 1⁄2 garlic powder
    • 2 tsp oregano
    Dry-mix Ingredients
    • 2 large chicken breasts
    • 4 eggs
    • 2 cups breadcrumbs or 2 cups crushed croutons

    Directions:

    1. Preheat oven at 375° F.

    2. In first bowl, combine 2 cups flour.

    3. In second bowl, whisk 4 eggs.

    4. In third bowl, put 1 Tbsp spice mix with 2 cups of breadcrumbs or crushed croutons.

    5. After patting dry the chicken, put chicken in first bowl until coated, second bowl until coated, then third bowl until coated.

    6. Cook, uncovered, for 1 hour.

    Recommendations:

  • Can be double dredged for extra crispiness, recommended double amount of wet ingredients.
