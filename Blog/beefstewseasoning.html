Beef Stew Seasoning

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp onion powder
  • 2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp celery seed
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1⁄2 tsp cayenne pepper

Recommendations:

  • Use 4 Tbsp per pound of beef stew

  • 2 cups of water in pressure cooker on STEW setting with a small bag of baby carrots, 3 large potatoes, 1 large onion sliced, and 1 large green pepper sliced

  • Optional: Can also be done by itself and served with cooked rice or noodles or pasta
