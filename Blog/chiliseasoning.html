Chili Seasoning

Ingredients:

  • 1⁄2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 1⁄2 Tbsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1⁄4 tsp pepper
  • 1⁄2 tsp oregano

Recommendations:

  • 1 Tbsp per pound of meat or veggies
  • Can be used with crumbled beef or shredded chicken or turkey
