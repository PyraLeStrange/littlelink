Fajita Seasoning

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1⁄2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp black pepper

Recommendations:

  • 1 Tbsp per pound of meat or veggies
  • Can be used for non-meat dishes
