KFC Chicken

Best baked chicken that you'll swear it's fried!

Ingredients:

  • Homemade KFC Seasoning Mix
  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs or 2 cups crushed croutons

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven at 375°.

  2. In first bowl, combine 2 cups flour.

  3. In second bowl, whisk 4 eggs.

  4. In third bowl, put 1 Tbsp spice mix with 2 cups of breadcrumbs or crushed croutons.

  5. After patting dry the chicken, put chicken in first bowl until coated, second bowl until coated, then third bowl until coated.

  6. Cook, uncovered, for 1 hour.

Recommendations:

  • Can be double dredged for extra crispiness, recommended double amount of wet ingredients.
